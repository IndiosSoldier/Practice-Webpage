The History of Cryptocurrency

You can not speak of the history of cryptocurrency, otherwise known as Crypto without speaking of Bitcoin. You can not speak of Bitcoin without speaking of Satoshi Nakamoto. So who is Satoshi Nakamoto? This is an exellent question. This is a question literraly every person involved in crypto wants to know. Satoshi Nakamoto is an illusive person or should we say an illusive persons? No one knows this either. Some speculate that Mr. Nokamoto is not a person at all but a group of people.

On August 18, 2008 the web domain bitcoin.org was registered. Then on October 31, 2009, Halloween Day, a paper authored by a Satoshi Nakamoto was sent to a cryptography mailing list. This paper was titled Bitcoin:A Peer-To-Peer Electronic Cash System. In January 2009 Nakamoto released and implemented the bitcoin software as a open-source code ensuring that bitcoin could not be monopolized by one individual or entity. This was the begining of decentralized banking and currency. On January 3, 2009 Nakamoto created the bitcoin network when he minned the starting block of the chain which is known as the geneisis block. This was the begining of the bitcoin block-chain. Upon the genesis block Nakamoto embeded a text that reads "The Times 03/Jan/2009 Chancellor on brink of second bailout for banks" Many speculate this was done in order to log the date and purpose of the bitcoin network which is to put currency into the peoples hands and out of the hands of centralized banking.
